Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Coloring & Highlights

How much does hair coloring or highlights typically cost at REDS Hair Salon JB in Skudai?
Our pricing for hair coloring and highlights varies based on hair length, desired technique (such as balayage, ombré, or full head highlights), and product choice. For short hair, basic coloring starts at RM150, while highlights start from RM180. For medium to long hair, prices can range from RM200 to RM400. We recommend booking a complimentary consultation at our City Square outlet in Skudai so our stylists can assess your hair and provide an accurate quote tailored to your needs.
How long does a typical coloring or highlighting appointment take at your salon?
The duration depends on the complexity of the service and your hair type. For single-process coloring, appointments usually take about 1.5 to 2 hours. For highlights, especially techniques like balayage or ombré, you should expect 2.5 to 4 hours. We recommend setting aside enough time, especially on weekends when our Skudai location can be busier.
Are your hair colorists licensed and experienced with international color brands?
Yes, our hair stylists are fully licensed and regularly trained in the latest coloring and highlighting techniques. We use reputable international color brands such as L'Oréal Professionnel and Wella, ensuring both quality results and hair health. Our team stays updated with current trends and attends ongoing education sessions.
What is the process for getting hair coloring or highlights at REDS Hair Salon JB?
First, you’ll have a consultation with one of our stylists to discuss your hair goals, color history, and any allergies. We’ll analyze your hair type and recommend the best coloring technique for your desired look. After sectioning and applying the color or highlights, we process your hair under professional supervision, rinse, and finish with a nourishing treatment. The stylist will also provide aftercare advice specific to the Johor climate to help your color last longer.
Do you offer any guarantees or touch-up policies if I’m not satisfied with my hair color?
Customer satisfaction is very important to us. If you’re not happy with your hair color or highlights, please contact us within 7 days of your appointment.
